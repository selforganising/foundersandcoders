Founders & Coders



 
What is Founders & Coders?
It is a full-time, eight-week coding course for people with no coding experience who want to become software developers or who want to build there own software-based startup.
How much does it cost?
It is free.
When does the it start?
March 31st, 2014
Who can apply?
Anyone over the age of 18, but we are giving priority to people aged 18-24 who live in Camden.
What are the entry requirements?
There are no academic requirements, but not everyone will be suited to the style of learning our courses require. You need to be self-motivated and committed to collaborative working. Coding also requires a deep curiosity about how things work and a certain stubbornness in the face of repeated failure.
What happens after the course ends?
You will join our alumni network. We encourage you to keep studying. We will provide you with continuing advice and support, and a place to study.
I want to work as a software developer. Can you help?
We are in the process of lining up a number of three-month paid internships with some of the best software companies in London.
I want to work on my own startup idea after the course. Can you help?
Alumni of the Academy who are serious about starting their own business are encouraged to apply for membership of Collective with all the benefits that provides, including access to a wider community of fellow founders and business mentors.
